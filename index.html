About the School

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1998

Dr. Zakir Hussain High School +2 is a premier academic institution in Bihar, committed to disciplined teaching, cultural values and a supportive learning environment. Our school combines tradition with strong student development programs for every class from VI to XII.

With a focus on Bihar Board benchmarks, character-building activities and attentive mentorship, we provide focused academic guidance while preserving respect, integrity and a calm campus experience.
