About the School

Dr. Zakir Hussain High School +2 is a traditional and respected educational institution in Patna, rooted in Bihar’s academic values and Hindi-medium heritage. Our school is known for a disciplined environment, deeply committed teachers, and a structured learning experience that prepares students for life beyond the classroom.

We combine rigorous Bihar Board curriculum standards with character-building activities, modern classrooms, and a focus on student development. Every child receives academic guidance and moral support in a caring atmosphere.

Our Commitment

  • Strong discipline and caring leadership
  • Quality education aligned with Bihar Board
  • Safe Hindi-medium environment for growth
  • Academic excellence with ethical values

Located near Sultanganj Police Station, our campus is convenient for local families and offers a calm setting for focused learning.

Principal’s Message
